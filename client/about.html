How It Works

Understanding Our Detection System

A powerful 4-step process that converts sign language gestures into text in real-time.

1

Hand Detection

MediaPipe identifies and tracks hand landmarks in real-time video feed with 21 key points per hand.

2

Feature Extraction

Landmark coordinates are normalized and processed into feature vectors for model input.

3

Classification

Neural network classifies the gesture into one of 37 possible signs (A-Z, 1-9).

4

Word Building

Detected letters are combined into words and sentences with intelligent timing algorithms.
